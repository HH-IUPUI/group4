The global classroom in collaboration with Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences (HH) and Indiana University–Purdue University Indianapolis (IUPUI) was divided into multiple groups. This website is created and designed by students from group 4 where, there are two students from IUPUI and two students from HH. This online collaboration course is a joint venture of Information Struct For The Web program at IUPUI and Orientation to Digital Services program at HH. Dr. William Helling and Kasper Valtakari are instructors from IUPUI and HH respectively. This website is basically the final project work of group 4 students.

Bio of Contributers
