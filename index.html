How are students coping with studying at their homes?

Overview

This is a brief survey of a few reactions to how students in higher education have been impacted by the pandemic around the globe based on a brief survey of article based around the globe. Some have faced severe physical, psychological, and economic challenges, while other have been able to improve certain circumstances about their lives. The authors of this page will also provide a brief glimpse into their own personal narratives about how they have survived and/or thrived during this unprecedented time.
